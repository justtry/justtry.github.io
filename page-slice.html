The iHeartCountry Festival on AT&T LIVE

Watch incredible performances from some of the biggest names in country music during the exclusive iHeartCountry Festival on AT&T LIVE in Austin, Texas. Check it out right here, right now, for free! The festival’s line up is packed solid with artists who have crushed the country music charts over the years, including such artists as Keith Urban; Thomas Rhett; Cole Swindell; Chris Young and Cassadee Pope; Sam Hunt; Blake Eldredge; Zac Brown Band; Lee Brice; and Miranda Lambert. Keith Urban, who has won Grammys, CMAs and Teen Choice Awards, opens up the show with his hit, “Wasted Time.” Which is his third single off of his latest album, Ripcord.
